The Journey

At the fine age of six, Li-Ming started following lessons for the recorder to develop the usefull skill of reading notes. After about 1.5 years he decided to switch to the piano. Following the example of his older sister Minke who'd been playing the piano for a couple years as well as singing. Li-Ming followed his lessons locally at 'Het KunstKwartier' in Helmond, The Netherlands. For about 10 years he'd had the same teach: Paul Chatrou who tought him almost everything he knows, with love and passion. Mr. Chatrou introduced Li-Ming to many genres especially classical caught his eye. After learning his first classical piece Für Elise, Li-Ming's passion for music was fully egnited. Now engulfing himself in Chopin's Nocturnes he has found more motivation than ever before. Mr. Chatrou stopped teaching after many many years and decided to focus on his other passion, composing piano music. Now Mrs. Marjola Lishi has taken Li-Ming under her wing for him to reach new unknown heights.
